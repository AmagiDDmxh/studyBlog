英语笔记-2

包括以下词汇

  1. Linguistics - 语言学
  2. Prelude - 前奏

词汇：

Linguistics [noun]

Meanings: The study of language in general and of particular languages, their structure, grammar, and history

记忆方法: Lin-guis-tic, 根据读音

e.g: Out with every theory of human behavior, from linguistics to sociology.

Linguistics 有很多的变形如：

去掉s【形容词】语言学的 - Linguistic [adjective]. e.g: She is pursuing her linguistic researches.

添加ian【名词】语言学家 - Linguistic-ian [noun]. e.g: The linguistician tolds us the rules of English grammar will help us a lot when writing some thesis and communicate with each other.

添加 ally 【副词】 语言学的 - Linguistic-ally [adverbial]. e.g: Linguistically, all languages are equally perfect and complete.

Prelude [noun, transitive verb, intransitive verb]

Meanings

  1. a prelude to sth

    if an event is a prelude to a more important event. it happens just before it and makes people expect it. (人们需要事先知道的某个重要事件的前奏、序幕、开端)

    e.g: Living together as a prelude to marriage is now considered acceptable in many countries. (婚前同居, 在如今许多国家都已经接受了)

  2. a short piece of music, especially one played at the beginning of a longer musical piece or before a church ceremony. (一小段的音乐，经常被用在长音乐作品的开始或教堂仪式之前)

    e.g: Chopin‘s Preludes (肖邦的前奏)

    e.g: an organ prelude (管风琴前奏曲)

记忆方法: prel-ude 根据读音, 也可以根据词根 pre-lude, 词根pre常有预先的意思
